Potato Salad

Description

A classic Chamorro side dish that is a must-have on your Fiesta table.

Ingredients

  • 5 pounds potatoes
  • 1 tablespoon onion powder
  • 2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 can chopped black olives, squeeze to remove as much liquid as possible
  • 2 jars diced pimentos, squeezed to remove as much liquid as possible
  • 1 jar sweet pickle relish, remove liquid
  • 3 cups mayonnaise

Steps

  1. Rinse the potatoes then place in a large pot fillrf with hot water. Bring the water to a boil. Cook until the potatoes are done. Remove cooked potatoes from the water; allow to cool completely then peel and discard the potato skin. Cut into small cubes.
  2. Place potatoes and eggs into a large mixing bowl.
  3. Add the onion powder, salt and black pepper. Stir gently to combine.
  4. Add the drained and squeezed olives, pimentos and relish to the bowl. Stir to combine.
  5. Fold in the mayonnaise. Taste and adjust the seasonings.
Serve and enjoy!
